My Journey So Far

Welcome to my personal space on the web! I'm Gagan Jha, a 20-year-old Psychology student at Ashoka University with a passion for understanding human behavior, and exploring creative pursuits. My journey has been shaped by diverse experiences that have helped me grow both academically and personally.

Growing up in Faridabad and studying at Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya laid the foundation for my academic interests. Those formative years taught me the value of perseverance and cultivated my curiosity about human potential. When I entered Ashoka University to pursue Psychology, I found myself drawn to both the scientific rigor of data analysis and the nuanced understanding of human behavior that psychology offers.

As I've progressed through my academic journey, I've discovered a deep satisfaction in helping others navigate their own paths. This has manifested in my work as a Student Advisor with the Lodha Genius Program and as a Campus Buddy at Ashoka, where I've had the privilege of mentoring students and supporting those with visual impairments.

Beyond academics, I find joy in creative expression through acting, participating in marathons, and exploring media production. These diverse interests inform my approach to psychology and enrich my understanding of human experience from multiple perspectives.

On this website, you'll find more about my academic journey, projects, and passions. I hope sharing my experiences might inspire others to embrace both analytical thinking and creative exploration in their own lives.
